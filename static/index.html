What Is an ATS Score — and Why Does It Matter?

Most companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to automatically screen resumes before a human ever reads them. These systems scan your resume for keywords, formatting, and relevance to the job description. If your score is too low, your application is rejected automatically — no matter how qualified you are.
