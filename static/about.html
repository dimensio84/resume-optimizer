Why We Built This

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) are the invisible gatekeepers of the modern job market. Employers use them to manage thousands of applications, and the systems work by scanning resumes for keyword matches. If your resume doesn't match the language of the job description closely enough, it's automatically rejected — regardless of how qualified you actually are.

The problem is that most job seekers don't know this is happening. They apply, hear nothing, and assume they weren't a good fit. In many cases, they were — they just used different words than the system was looking for.

We built ResumeChangers to solve this specific problem: take your real experience, rewrite it using the language that matches the job you're applying for, and give you a clear score so you know exactly where you stand.

How It Works

You paste your resume and the job description you're applying for. Our AI — powered by Claude, Anthropic's large language model — analyzes the gap between the two. It identifies which keywords and phrases the ATS is likely scanning for, rewrites your resume to include them naturally, and generates a tailored cover letter for the role.

The whole process takes under 60 seconds. You get back an ATS compatibility score (0–100), a list of missing keywords, your fully rewritten resume, and a ready-to-send cover letter.

The price is $7.99 per optimization — a single, one-time charge with no subscription, no auto-renewal, and no hidden fees. Payment is processed securely by Stripe.

The Technology

ResumeChangers is powered by Claude, the AI assistant developed by Anthropic. We chose Claude specifically because of its ability to understand the nuance of career language — not just matching keywords mechanically, but rewriting experience in a way that reads naturally to both automated systems and human hiring managers.

Claude excels at understanding long, complex documents — which matters when you're comparing a multi-page resume against a detailed job description. It produces writing that sounds like a confident, experienced professional, not a generic AI output.
