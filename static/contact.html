Common Questions

How do I get a refund?

Email us at support@resumechangers.com with your purchase email address and we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked. We process refunds within 1–2 business days and they typically appear on your statement within 5–10 days depending on your bank.

I paid but didn't receive my results — what do I do?

This is usually caused by a browser session timing out during processing. Email us with the email address used for your purchase and the approximate time of your transaction — we can look up your results and resend them, or issue a full refund.

Can I run the same resume against multiple job descriptions?

Yes — each optimization is a separate $7.99 purchase. Because each job description has different keywords the ATS scans for, a new optimization gives you a fresh rewrite tailored to that specific role.

Is my resume data stored anywhere?

No. Your resume text and job description are processed in-memory and not stored on our servers beyond the session. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Do you offer bulk or team pricing?

We don't currently offer bulk pricing, but if you have a specific need — for example, a career coach optimizing resumes for multiple clients — email us at support@resumechangers.com and we can discuss options.
